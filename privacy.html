Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 14, 2026

File Manager & Backup PRO ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Data Accessed

Our app accesses your local files to display them to you and uses the Google Drive API to perform backups at your request.

2. Data Storage

We do not collect or store your files on our own servers. Your files are only stored locally on your device or in your own Google Drive account.

3. Third-Party Services

We use Google OAuth for authentication and the Google Drive API for storage. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.
